mm - Millimeter

Millimeters are a unit of length that is equal to 1/1000 of a meter. It is used in typography to define the size of a font. It is also used in the printing industry to define the size of a printed page. Millimeters are commonly used in web design to define the size of text and other elements on a web page. Millimeters are a relative unit of length, which means that the size of a millimeter is not fixed and can vary depending on the resolution of the screen or printer.
