cm - Centimeter

Centimeters are a unit of length that is equal to 1/100 of a meter. It is used in typography to define the size of a font. It is also used in the printing industry to define the size of a printed page. Centimeters are commonly used in web design to define the size of text and other elements on a web page. Centimeters are a relative unit of length, which means that the size of a centimeter is not fixed and can vary depending on the resolution of the screen or printer.
