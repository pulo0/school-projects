em

The em is a unit of length that is equal to the computed font size of the element. It is used in typography to define the size of a font. The em is a relative unit of length, which means that the size of an em is not fixed and can vary depending on the font size of the parent element. The em is commonly used in web design to define the size of text and other elements on a web page.
